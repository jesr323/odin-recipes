Protein Shakes


Three of the best Protein Shakes for muscle gain!


Whether you’re looking for a post-workout protein shake or a new protein smoothie to switch up your regular routine, we’ll teach you how to easily make a protein shake with 30+g protein in it.


Ingredients

  • Fresh Strawberries.

  • Hemp seeds.

  • Chia seeds.

  • Flax seeds.

  • Nut butter.

  • Milk or Plant milk or Almond milk.

Steps

  1. First grab 3/4 cup of fresh or frozen StrawBerries throw them into the blender.

  2. Second add around 200ml of water or milk on to the blender with the StrawBerries.(Plant milk or Almond milk works great too!)

  3. Third grab a small amout of each Flex seeds, Chia seeds, and Hemp seeds throw them it into the blender.

  4. Last grab some Nut butter or My choice Peanut Butter.

  5. Now blend and thats it.
